# Responsible Use

This project can create realistic face-swapped video. Use it only when you have a lawful, ethical, and consent-based reason to do so.

## Allowed Uses

- Work with your own face or media you have permission to edit.
- Create clearly labeled parody, satire, education, research, accessibility, or visual-effects material.
- Restore or transform personal media where all relevant people have consented.
- Test detection, watermarking, moderation, or media-forensics workflows.

## Do Not Use This Project To

- Create or distribute non-consensual intimate imagery.
- Impersonate someone in a deceptive, fraudulent, political, financial, or reputationally harmful way.
- Harass, threaten, shame, exploit, or mislead people.
- Bypass platform policies, identity checks, biometric systems, or content safeguards.
- Process private or copyrighted media without appropriate permission.

## Disclosure

When publishing generated output, clearly disclose that the media was altered or synthesized. Keep the disclosure close to the media, especially when viewers could mistake the output for authentic footage.

## Consent And Privacy

Before using a person's face, confirm you have permission for the specific use, audience, and distribution channel. Delete source images, intermediate frames, and generated videos when they are no longer needed.

## No Warranty

The software is provided as-is. You are responsible for complying with applicable laws, platform rules, and the rights of people represented in source and target media.
